AI paper-trading simulation · data snapshot Jul 10, 2026 · 1:20 PM ET

A self-driving $100k paper portfolio — scanned, scored, and stop-guarded on a market-day clock.

Stock.AI sweeps ~700 tickers daily, has Gemini score a 20-name shortlist, and trades a simulated account with four layered stop-losses. A built-in scheduler runs light sell-only checks all morning and the full pipeline each afternoon — no button-pressing required.

Risk engine

Four layered stops on every position

Checked five times each morning and at every Recommend. First match wins; all fills modeled with liquidity-aware slippage.
